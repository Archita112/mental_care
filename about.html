Mental peace refers to the deliberate state of spiritual calm and the
potential of stressors such as the burden arising from pretending to
perform at an optional level with a positive mind (inner peace).
Peace of mind is generally associated with joy, happiness, calmness,
prayer, yoga, meditation etc.

Mental health includes emotional, psychological, and social well-being.
It affects how we think, feel, act, make choices, and relate to others.
Mental health is more than the absence of a mental illness— it's essential
to your overall health and quality of life.
Many spiritual practices refer to this peace as an
experience of knowing oneself.
But we all know that inner peace is important to mental health.
The human beings who are mentally strong are confident that they
can handle whatever life throws their way.
